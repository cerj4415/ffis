A Free Online Computer Science & IT Bootcamp

Hey, I'm Cerj, and this is my free online bootcamp guide. This guide is for students, professionals and those seeking a career in technology. The content posted here is meant to serve as a primary or supplemental learning resource. All of the guide's content is valuable and meant to be studied in order, but personally irrelevant subjects can be skipped. Overall, this guide is meant to prepare for and begin a career in software development or information technology, by studying web/app/game develpoment, data science, networking and security. Even if you just have an app or game idea and need some guidance, this is a great place to start. I'm here to help, so be sure to bookmark this page, follow on social media and leave a comment. Become a part of the IT community and let us know what goal you're trying to achieve.
